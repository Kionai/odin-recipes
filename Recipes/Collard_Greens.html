Collard Greens

Alright honey! Now that you've mastered the art of the souther mac and cheese, its time to graduate to another souther delicacy: Collard Greens!

Intro To Collards

While they may seem easy enough to make on first glance ( Its a Green vegetable. How hard could it be? Right?) WRONG! Achieving a perfectly balanced and flavorful collard green is an art. Good thing you have me here to guide you through. Let's get started!

Ingredients

  • 1 tbl olive oil
  • 1 small white onion
  • 3 cloves of garlic (minced)
  • 1 tsp read pepper flakes
  • 1 large smoked turkey leg
  • 32 oz collard greens thoroughly washed and chopped
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • hot sauce optional

Ingredients

  1. In a large, deep skillet or pot, heat olive oil on medium heat
  2. Add in onions and cook until tender
  3. Add chicken broth, red pepper flakes and smoked turkey
  4. Bring to a boil and reduce heat
  5. Cover and boil lightly for about 20-30 minutes
  6. Remove turkey leg and let it cool
  7. Remove meat from bones and cut into bite-sized pieces
  8. Return meat and skin back to the pot
  9. Simmer for 10 minutes
  10. Add collard greens
  11. When greens begin to wilt down, cover and simmer for up to 60 minutes or until your desired texture is reached, stirring occasionally
  12. Add salt and pepper if desired
  13. Plate the greens and add a few drops of hot sauce
  14. Serve hot
  15. ENJOY!!
