Southern Mac & Cheese

Let me walk you through the joys of making the most delicious mac & cheese!

Delicious southern mac & cheese. Recipe inspired by the geniuses over at Divas Can Cook.com

People tend to keep a tight grasp on their prized macaroni & cheese recipes, but not in my family! We share our favorite recipes like they're going out of style! Which is why I'm going to share this tried and true recipe with you today!

Ya'll , this baked macaroni and cheese is FULL OF SOUL! That is the first thing that came to mind when I took that first bite. Usually I'm not that into mac & cheese recipes but this one? Yea, this one gets a pass! So let's get started!

Ingredients

  • 3 cups elbow macaroni (uncooked)
  • 1 1/2 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 1 cup Colby Jack, shredded
  • 6-8 0z Velveeta cheese
  • 1 cup sharp cheddar
  • sal & pepper to taste
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup smoked cheddar ** trust me you don't want to skip this **
  • paprika (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 F
  2. Cook macaroni until just al dente or a little under al dente. Look on the back of the box to see how long you need to cook your pasta for it to reach al dente
  3. Drain pasta and set aside
  4. In a large bowl, add milk, heavy cream and cheeses. (Except for the smoked cheddar)
  5. Stir to combine
  6. Taste the milk mixture and add salt & pepper as needed
  7. When you're content wth the taste, add the eggs
  8. Stir well until combined
  9. Butter a 9X9 inch banking dish
  10. Add macaroni into the baking dish
  11. Pour cheese mixture over macaroni
  12. Make sure the cheese is distributed well
  13. Top with the smoked cheddar. Note: This is when you can get fancy! Add a host of other cheeses if you'd like!
  14. Bake for 35-45 minutes. Do not overbake. It may be a bit jiggly when you take it out of the oven but it will firm as it cools.
  15. Let cool for about 10-15 minutes
  16. Enjoy!
